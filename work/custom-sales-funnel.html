The
Problem

Social followers don't automatically become customers.

"Likes don't pay the bills."

The Context

The client had built a significant social media following but was struggling to convert that audience into revenue.

Their existing link-in-bio sent followers to a generic landing page with a high drop-off rate and no personalisation.

The Opportunity

A quiz-based funnel would let us segment the audience by their specific needs, then route each person to the product or offer most relevant to them.

Personalisation at scale — without needing a sales team.

The Hypothesis

If we replace a generic landing page with a short, engaging quiz that segments users and surfaces the right offer, we can dramatically increase the conversion rate without increasing ad spend.

Research
& Strategy

Understanding the audience before designing the experience.

Audience Research
  • Segmentation: Identified 3 distinct audience personas based on comments, DMs, and poll data from the client's social channels.
  • Pain Points: Each segment had a different primary problem the client's products could solve.
  • Drop-off Analysis: Audited the existing landing page to identify where and why users were leaving.
Funnel Strategy

Designed a 5-question quiz to identify each visitor's segment, then route them to a tailored results page with a personalised offer and CTA.

Kept the quiz short enough to maintain engagement, but long enough to gather meaningful data for segmentation.

Design
& Build

Matching the client's brand while optimising for conversion.

Design Decisions
  • Brand Alignment: The quiz matched the client's existing visual identity so the transition from social felt seamless.
  • Progress Indicator: A visible progress bar reduced abandonment by giving users a sense of completion.
  • Mobile-First: The majority of traffic came from Instagram, so every interaction was designed for thumbs first.
The Build

Built the quiz logic in Typeform and the surrounding funnel pages in Webflow, connecting them via embed and custom redirects based on quiz outcomes.

Each results page was a bespoke landing page with a personalised headline, social proof relevant to that segment, and a single, clear CTA.

Results
& Learnings

The Outcome

The quiz funnel outperformed the original landing page significantly, with a higher conversion rate and lower cost-per-acquisition across the client's product range.

The segmentation data also gave the client valuable insight into their audience's needs for future product development.

Key Learnings
  • Personalisation Works: Routing users to relevant offers, rather than a generic page, made the biggest difference.
  • Brevity Matters: Quiz completion dropped with each additional question — 5 was the sweet spot.
  • Mobile is Everything: Over 80% of traffic arrived on mobile. Desktop was an afterthought.
